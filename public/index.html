  • Summary panel — all upcoming assignments sorted by date, color-coded
  • Drag-to-reorder groups inside Settings
  • Edit button (✎) on each assignment reopens the pre-filled form
  • Per-group quick + Add button in the class header
  • Data persists across updates — db.json is git-ignored

  • Homework tracking with optional deadlines and notes

Terms & Privacy

StudyFlow is a personal homework scheduler built for students.

Your Data

Everything you enter — classes, assignments, deadlines — is stored only on your own device in a local file (data/db.json). We do not have servers that collect your information.

No Tracking

StudyFlow does not use analytics, advertising, or any third-party tracking. We don't know who you are, what classes you take, or how you use the app.

No Accounts

There are no accounts, no sign-ins, and no passwords. Your data never leaves your machine unless you choose to share the file yourself.

We don’t collect or sell your data — we only use it to show your schedule.
