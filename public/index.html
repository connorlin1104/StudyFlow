Terms & Privacy

StudyFlow is a personal scheduler built for students.

Your Data

Everything you enter — tabs, classes, assignments, deadlines — is stored in Google Firestore under your account. Only you can read or write your own data; Firestore security rules block all cross-user access.

Sign-in

StudyFlow uses Google Sign-In via Firebase Auth. We only receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google to display in the Account panel. We do not store passwords.

No Tracking

StudyFlow does not use analytics, advertising, or any third-party tracking beyond Firebase services (Auth and Firestore). We do not sell your data or share it with anyone.

Data Deletion

Deleting a tab removes all its classes and assignments from Firestore immediately. To delete your entire account and all associated data, use the Schedule Transfer page to export first, then contact us.

Your data is stored in your Google account — we don’t sell or share it.
